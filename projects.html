Objectives: The interventions aim to ensure that AGYW in Bomachoge Chache Sub County have access to the essential package of biomedical, behavioral and structural interventions above to reduce their vulnerability to HIV infection through:

Reaching 279,407 AGYW with defined package of services to support HIV prevention and risk reduction.

Ongoing

Increasing linkage of AYP living with HIV to treatment, care and support services through structure peer support approaches to improve the county achievement of the 90-90-90 targets.

Ongoing

Increasing the involvement of young people and youth focused organizations in driving demand for increased uptake and adherence among AGYW.

Ongoing

Contributing to the reduction in STI’s and HIV infections among AGYW.

Ongoing

To contribute to reduction of HIV risk amongst AGYW within the selected sub-counties .

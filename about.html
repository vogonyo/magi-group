History

Magi was incorporated on 19th August 2014. Having started as table banking informal youth group for college students, there was need to register formally. Magi is a youth led organization, that focuses on young women, men and the disabled in the society, through building capacities.

Organizational Capacities

Magi has an active Board of Directors and Management team with a Chief Executive Officer, Head of Programmes, Programmes Officer, Finance & Accounts Officer, Monitoring and Evaluation Officer, trained and qualified Nurse, Associate Counsellors and Trainers, Corporate and Communication officer, Community Resource personnel, Business and Sustainable Community Development Officer, Occupational health and Safety officer (OHS), Head of Curriculum Development as well as other Support staff. The associate members are professionals in different fields, who are assigned tasks based on their areas of specialization.
